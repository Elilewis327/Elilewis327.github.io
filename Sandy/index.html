General info
Hurricane Sandy, sometimes known as superstorm Sandy, was a massive weather event in october of 2012. The effects of the event include 233 casualties and around $70 billion in economic damages, Making it one of the top 10 most economically damaging storms since 1900 in the US. The event caused storm surges and other flooding, as well as other extreme weather events. While it had downgraded into a 70 kt tropical cyclone by the time it hit the US coast, the sheer size of the storm caused it to have a massive impact. See the size, damages, and more below.
